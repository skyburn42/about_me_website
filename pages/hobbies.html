My Top Hobbies

Photography

I used to love Photography and it used to be something that I wanted to really get into when I was younger, but realized it would be better to be just a past time hobby. Nature and Wildlife photograpy is my thing!

Hiking

Hiking is my favorite way to be able to get a good exercise in, while being outdoors discovering new trails and hidden scenic views that nature has for me. My favorite hikes are when there is a nice waterfall or lake to see at the end.

Boating

Boating has only become a recent top favorite hobby of mine, and that is soley because I learned how to wakeboard! Getting up on the water on a nice hot day is something I can do all summer long. Unless there is a camping trip planned. Camping trumps boating any day

Camping

There is nothing better then to be able to sit by a campfire at night, with nothing obscuring your view of the night sky. Camping is so relaxing and is my favorite way to spend a weekend vacation away from civilization, roast some marshmallows, and listen to the silent sounds of the trees, nightlife, and the crackling of the fire. (This is the only picture I have of a campfire and roasting marshmallows, at my house so not technically camping but you get the idea!)
